On the recommendation of someone I admire, I started to read The Michael Neill's book Inside Out Revolution: The only thing you need to know to change your life forever."
I've struggled with it. Not because I don't agree with the content but because it's truly challenging stuff, and it pushes me outside of my intellectual comfort zone.

The simple truth is this: Our experience of life is created from the inside out... We're living in the feeling of our thinking not the feeling of the world.

or

...frustration isn't feedback about our life but feedback about the quality of our thinking at any particular moment.
