On the flip side of my earlier post about the importance of giving thanks for other people's care and brilliance, it seemed important to reflect on receiving other people's feedback.
I'm in the very fortunate position of getting lots of feedback from people. But some of it is really hard to take, and I struggle to accept what people are saying. As with a lot of the people I work with the problem is not accepting critical feedback but hearing the positives.
Even typing this I want to bat myself round the back of the head. I mean basically I've just announced that people say nice things about me, I mean, really, how much do I love myself?
